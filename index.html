the  doomsday  rule

A mathematical party-trick to calculate the weekday for (almost) any given date.
This app just demonstrates the step by step calculations and provides a practice mode.
Please refer to the Wikipedia article for an in-depth description of the method and variations.

Steps for 12/7/2021

Century doomsday

Calculate or memorize the weekday of the century doomsday.

cd=Tuesday−(⌊yyyy100⌋mod  4)∗2cd = Tuesday - ({\lfloor}\frac{{yyyy}}{100}{\rfloor} \mod 4) * 2cd=Tuesday−(⌊100yyyy​⌋mod4)∗2
=Tuesday−(⌊2021100⌋mod  4)∗2= Tuesday - ({\lfloor}\frac{{2021}}{100}{\rfloor} \mod 4) * 2=Tuesday−(⌊1002021​⌋mod4)∗2
=2−(20mod  4)∗2= 2 - (20 \mod 4) * 2=2−(20mod4)∗2
=2−0∗2= 2 - 0 * 2=2−0∗2
=2−0= 2 - 0=2−0
=2= 2=2

Century doomsday is a Tuesday (2).

Year doomsday

Calculate the doomsday's weekday of the year, based on the last 2 digits of the target year yy and the century doomsday cd.

(⌊yy12⌋+yymod  12+⌊yymod  124⌋+cd)mod  7({\lfloor}\frac{yy}{12}{\rfloor} + yy \mod 12 + {\lfloor}\frac{yy \mod 12}{4}{\rfloor} + cd) \mod 7(⌊12yy​⌋+yymod12+⌊4yymod12​⌋+cd)mod7
=(⌊2112⌋+21mod  12+⌊21mod  124⌋+2)mod  7= ({\lfloor}\frac{21}{12}{\rfloor} + 21 \mod 12 + {\lfloor}\frac{21 \mod 12}{4}{\rfloor} + 2) \mod 7=(⌊1221​⌋+21mod12+⌊421mod12​⌋+2)mod7

=14mod  7= 14 \mod 7=14mod7
=0= 0=0

The year's doomsday weekday is a Sunday (0). Remember this weekday for the last step.

Doomsday dates

Remember the doomsday dates for any given year and select the one that is closest to your date:

  • 03.01. (or 04.01. for leap years)
  • last day of February
  • π day
  • "I work 9-to-5 at 7-Eleven"
  • twin dates for 4, 6, 8, 10, 12

This means you have the following dates as doomsdays for your year:

The closest doomsday date is the 12/12/2021.

Approaching target date

Approach the target date from the closest doomsday date in 7-day steps.

12/1212/1212/12

The closest date with a doomsday weekday is the 12/12/2021.

Difference to target date

Calculate the difference in days from the closest date with a doomsday weekend to your target date.

closest→input=Δ daysclosest \rightarrow input = \Delta \ daysclosest→input=Δ days
12/12/2021→12/7/2021=−5 days12/12/2021 \rightarrow 12/7/2021 = -5 \ days12/12/2021→12/7/2021=−5 days

The difference is -5 days.

Result

Take the year's doomsday weekday and add the day difference. If the difference is negative, you can add it to 7 and add that resulting value, e.g. +1+1+1 instead of −6-6−6.

yd+Δ=resultyd + \Delta = resultyd+Δ=result
Sunday (0)−5=Tuesday (2)Sunday\ (0) - 5 = Tuesday\ (2) Sunday (0)−5=Tuesday (2)
Sunday (0)+2=Tuesday (2)Sunday\ (0) + 2 = Tuesday\ (2) Sunday (0)+2=Tuesday (2)

You have your final result! The 12/7/2021 is a Tuesday.
